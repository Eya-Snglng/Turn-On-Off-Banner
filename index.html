What Do I Look for in a Partner?

TURN ONS

Forget the butterflies in your stomach, let's talk about fireworks! The qualities that make my soul sing in a partner is when...

  • ...they light up a conversation with their knowledge and passion
  • ...they surprise me with their adventurous and sponty side
  • ...they are my biggest cheerleader, celebrating my wins and offering support
  • ...they inspire my creativity and make me want to be a better version of myself
  • ...they look effortlessly amazing even with bedhead

TURN OFFS

Ditch the dealbreakers, we all have those quirks that make us raise an eyebrow! The traits that would have me swiping left on a dating app is when...

  • ...they make me pay for the date. need I explain more?
  • ...they are a major mood-killer who constantly complains about everything
  • ...they can't put their phone down when we're together
  • ...they text constantly and doesn't respect my time
  • ...they boast about their wealth too much
